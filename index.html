NearGYM works in four simple steps

1-Search

It´s simple. On NearGYM homepage, enter the name of a GYM you are interested for and click on search. Alternatively click on the search link on the top menu to view the list of all GYMS.

2-Locate

Get to see where the school is located by clicking on the GYM profile. It´s even more exciting. You con get directions from anywhere in the country.

3-Register

Register in the GYM of your choice. Click on the REGISTER button to get the register form. We also follow up on the register to make sure you get a reply on time.

4-Train

Once you get the answer. You can now start training on the schedule that the gym have and get a new healthy life.
